When this plugin sends data to SW Statistics?

The first - and the most important - data upload is when you launch the game. This plugin sends your whole profile data to the server, which saves only the important information. Then, everytime you do some action that triggers one of the commands SW Statistics Plugin printed for you in SWEX, it sends its command data. Mostly Dungeon Runs & Siege defenses. In order to send your siege defense records, you need to go to Siege and click on Defense. Every other command is sent without doing additional tasks (just by playing).
If SW Statistics Website doesn't have your profile saved, it may throw some errors or give you a message in SWEX that server couldn't handle this. The best you can do is to let me know AND also restart Summoners War.

Siege Defenses

In order to upload Siege Defenses record, you need to go to Siege Battle screen, then tap on Defenses. It is also possible to upload friend's Siege Defenses, by going to Members and then tapping on specific friend.
